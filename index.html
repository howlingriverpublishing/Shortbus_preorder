About the Book

"Ain't No Bus Fare for the Short Bus" is a gripping and vulnerable exploration of identity, adversity, and triumph. Micah O. Briggs Sr. weaves reflective storytelling with the grit and pulse of hip-hop culture, offering a powerful narrative that speaks to anyone who has ever had to fight their way through.

What Readers Can Expect

  • Raw, unfiltered poetry and storytelling
  • Deep emotional introspection
  • Honest reflections on youth, mistakes, and redemption
  • Hip-hop inspired rhythm and voice
